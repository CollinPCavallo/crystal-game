In this game, a random number from 1-100 will be generated and be displayed on "Number to Guess". You have 4 crystals, each one holds a different value that is hidden until it is clicked on, and then added to your score. It is up to you to try to get your score to match the random number generated and get the win. If you go over, you lose. Have Fun :D
